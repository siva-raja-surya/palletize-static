About Company

We at Palletizex are committed to the sustainable future of our planet. Our goal is to provide an environmentally-friendly option for your supply chain.

Our team at Palletizex has innovated a way to mass manufacture Pallets using Agri-waste as the main raw material. Carbon footprint of our Pallets is amongst the lowest of all materials. In addition to the clear sustainability benefits, our Pallets demonstrate versatility in functional warehouse applications. Enhanced strength, durability, moisture resistance, and termite resistance make our pallets ideal for warehouse operations all year round.

Sustainability has long been an issue in the existing wood pallet industry as more customers are demanding attention to sustainability in both the wood species used and its origin. Supply chains across the globe are in need of climate-neutral pallets that are nature-based and can claim a function out of nature.

Palletizex Green-Tech Pvt Ltd is providing the most sustainable pallets for the active market with its novel & patent-pending technology. With more than 3 billion wooden pallets being used annually, leading to deforestation of over 250 million trees, our innovation enables a sustainable revolution across the supply chain.

Turn sustainability commitments into action with our Bio-Pallets

Organizations are facing the need to modify conventional behaviours in order to stabilise our future and accelerate the transition to a global net-zero carbon economy.

Pallets are ready to carry more weight in the discourse as firms go deeper into their supply chains in search of methods to decrease waste, energy use, greenhouse gas emissions, and other negative impacts.

Our mission is to offer the global supply chain ecologically friendly pallets. Palletizex has developed a method for mass-producing pallets from agri-waste as a raw material.

Our sustainable pallets enable faster, broader transformation for enterprises at various stages of their sustainability journey while balancing growth and impact — a moment we’ve been working towards.

Our pallets have one of the smallest carbon footprints of any material. In addition to environmental benefits, they offer enhanced strength, durability, moisture resistance, and termite resistance — making them excellent for warehouse operations all year.

Climate-neutral pallets that are nature-based are needed across global supply chains. With more than 6 billion wooden pallets produced worldwide — resulting in deforestation of over 400 million trees — our solution drives sustainable transformation.

Palletizex is inspired by the opportunity to help organizations make meaningful, sustainable progress.
